IU School of Dentistry

Welcome! We represent the IU School of Dentistry. We have been providing care in the Student Outreach Clinic since 2011. Our student volunteers come from the Doctor of Dental Surgery Program in all four years. We are able to provide dental treatment under the supervision of our volunteer faculty. At the clinic, we treat urgent and acute needs, including dental pain, orofacial pain, swellings, and trauma. Common treatments we perform include oral examinations, restorations (fillings), and extractions.

How to get involved as a student

IU School of Dentistry students enrolled in the Doctor of Dental Surgery Program may volunteer at the clinic. Please check your school email from the IUSD SOC student board for instructions on how to volunteer.
