IU School of Medicine Opthamology

IUSM's OSIG operates the eye clinic on he last Saturday of each month (pending holidays). We offer a multitude of eye health screening for a near-comprehensive exam including intraocular pressures, visual field, acuity, and refraction measurements along with fundus imaging. Our faculty and resident volunteers review our screening results and determine the need for referrals to Eskenazi for further workup and management, and our team of patient navigators provide resources and communication to help ensure proper follow-up so that our neighbors can get the full eye care they need. We have also partnered with a local LensCrafters so that our neighbors in need of eyeglasses can receive a free pair of frames with prescription lenses using our referral!

Info about how to get involved as a student
