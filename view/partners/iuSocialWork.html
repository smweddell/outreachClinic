IU School of Social Work

Social Work joined the clinic in 2011 with a mission to foster social and economic change in the Near Eastside by building relationships with community members, collaborating with existing networks, increasing awareness of health disparities, and promoting education while improving the skills of social work students to understand the strengths, dignity, and hope in all.

How to get involved as a student

BSW and MSW students currently enrolled in the IU School of Social Work can volunteer. Volunteer hours at the clinic may count toward your practicum hours. Please Email: iusoc.ssw@gmail.com to learn more and to fill out a new volunteer form!
